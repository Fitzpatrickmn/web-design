Mapping Maximum Temperature vs Latitude

Out of all the graphs, the temperature graph (determined by the max-temperature) shows the strongest correlation to latitude. Temperature increases as we near the equator and get away from latitudes higher than 60-degrees. Temperature dips slightly as we reach -60-degrees latitude as well. This would be due to the average temperature at the north and south poles.
